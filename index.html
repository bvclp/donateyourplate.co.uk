No child should go hungry over half term

This half-term, thousands of children in Blyth Valley will be denied a vital free school meal voucher.

This could have been avoided.

Our local MP, Ian Levy, voted against a temporary extension to Free School Meal vouchers, which would have included the school holiday periods until April 2021, to help our hardest hit children face the ongoing COVID-19 crisis. Ian Levy MP has stated publicly he does not believe our children should benefit from this targeted help when schools are closed.

Each school meal voucher is worth just £3.

They provide a lifeline for thousands of families in Blyth Valley and ensure our children who are impacted by COVID are able to eat.

We demand that Ian Levy, MP for our bairns, changes direction.

WE CALL FOR ACTION

WE CALL ON IAN LEVY, MP FOR BLYTH VALLEY, TO SUPPORT EXTENDED FREE SCHOOL MEALS

We’re calling for the people of Blyth Valley to tell Ian Levy MP, he must support his constituency on this issue and not just the Conservative Party.
